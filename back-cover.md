Most people approach **Leviticus** expecting a book of ancient laws, unfamiliar rituals, and difficult passages. Few expect to discover one of the Bible's most beautiful invitations.

**Drawing Near** is a 50-day contemplative devotional that uncovers the spiritual heart of Leviticus through the light of Christ. Rather than reading the book as a collection of regulations, these reflections reveal the quiet thread woven through every sacrifice, festival, Sabbath, priestly blessing, and act of worship: **God's enduring desire to dwell with His people.**

Each day's reading follows the movement of Scripture—from the Garden of Eden to the Tabernacle, from the Temple to Christ, from the Church to the hope of the New Creation—showing how the symbols of Leviticus continue to speak to our own journey of faith today.

Written in a gentle, reflective style inspired by the Christian contemplative tradition, these meditations invite you to move beyond fear, obligation, and performance into a deeper awareness of God's faithful presence. Along the way, you will discover that holiness is not primarily about keeping distance from God, but learning to live in ever-deepening communion with Him.

Whether Leviticus has long been a mystery to you or a familiar companion, **Drawing Near** offers a fresh way of reading one of Scripture's most overlooked books—one that transforms ancient symbols into living invitations to trust, rest, worship, and love.

Come, take another step toward the God who has been drawing near to you all along.
